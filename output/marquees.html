Marquee Museum

Welcome to my <marquee> museum!

The <marquee> element is from a different time in browser history, when it was more acceptable to make websites with whimsy. These days, bleeding hearts accuse it of being user-hostile and discourage its use, but every major web browser continues to support it. This is my field journal of <marquee> tags still roaming the wild web.
