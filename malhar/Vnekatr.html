यूनिवर्सिटी ऑफ़ मिशिगन

Translation exercise on linking verb to verb with V-ने का and V-ने की.

            Translate into Hindi. Use V-ने का  or V-ने की  wherever possible.
1. The farmers tried to clear ( साफ़ कर ) the wells before the rainy season बरसात f ).
2. The army has gotten the order to stop all tourists.
3. They must be in a hurry to fill the boat.
4. Wait for the right moment ( घड़ी f ) to come.  Then we'll show our strength.
5. Maybe somebody is thinking of opening a new shop here.
6. She began to be sorry she stayed.
(An earlier version of this translation exercise appears in § 9B of  Hindi Structures 1979, p 77.)
