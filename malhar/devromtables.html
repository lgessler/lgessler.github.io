      This page contains tables of equivalents or correspondences that hold between the Xdvng font symbols and the underlying source code.
   
Vowels

   अ = A    अा = Aa    इ = E    ई = I

   उ = u    ऊ = U    ऋ = ? or & # 256 ;    ए = O    ऐ = O & # 240 ;

   अाे = A a & # 240 ;    अाै = A a & # 242 ;

   
Matras

   ह = h    हा = h a    हि = e h    ही = h i

   हु = h & # 216 ;    हू = h & # 220 ;    हृ = & # 198 ;   ( पृ = p: & # 224 ; )   हे = h & # 240 ;    है = h & # 242 ;

   हाे = h a & # 240 ;    हाै = h a & # 242 ;
