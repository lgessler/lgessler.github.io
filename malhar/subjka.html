15A.  ka of subject.

    In Hindi, if a verb is not finite, that is, if it is not altered for number, gender or person, then its subject is usually marked with ka :

Its subject, therefore, is marked with kaH Es: dðv:t:a ka Aan:a , literally 'the coming of this deity'.     'But the priests are afraid of his being born on Earth.'
