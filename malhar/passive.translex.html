Review of passive: translation exercise.

    Translate into Hindi. Use the passive where possible.
1.    A tika ( टीका  m 2) can be applied with the thumb.
2.    You don't tell a person this to his face.
3.    Don't put those bottles here. Otherwise, they'll deliberately ( जान-बूझकर ) be broken ( तोड़ ).
4.    By then Nixon had already been implicated ( X का नाम ले ) in the Watergate affair.
5.    Every day the trash is taken away at six o'clock.
6.    Okay, we can sit ( बैठकर ) here and take a quick look ( नज़र ) at the examination result.
7.    She's to be seen ( साथ दे ) to her door.
