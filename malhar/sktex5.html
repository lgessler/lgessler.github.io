 A. Answer the following questions.   (Use  सन्धि  where appropriate.)
1.  त्वं केन सह वससि  ?
    With my son.  

    With my father.  

    With my horse.  

    With my horses.  

    With a hero.  

    With servants.  

    With (two) servants.  

    With the king.  

    With an elephant.  

 
2.  तत्रभवानिदं सर्वम् कस्मै यच्छति  ?     [ इदम्  =  'this';   सर्वम्  =  'all' ]  
    To my son.  

    To my father.  

    To my horse.  

    To my horses.  

    To a friend.  

    To my servants.  

    To my (two) servants.  

    To the king.  

    To the people.  

 
3.  भवान् कस्मादिदं हरति  ?       [ हृ  /  हरति   'take' (Class I, परस्मैपद ) ]
    From my son.  

    From my father.  

    From my friends.  

    From a friend.  

    From my (two) friends.  

    From my servants.  

    From my (two) servants.  

    From the king.  

    From the people.  

 
4.  इदं कस्य भवति  ?  
    (This) is my son's.  

    (This) is my father's.  

    (This) is my friend's.  

    (This) is my friends'.  

    (This) is my (two) friends'.  

    (This) is my servant's.  

    (This) is my friend's servant's.  

    (This) is the king's.  

    (This) is the people's.  

 
5.  तव मित्रं क्व वसति  ?     [ क्व  is another word for 'where' ]  
    (He) lives in a palace.  

    (He) lives in the woods.  

    (He) lives in a house.  

    (He) lives in a well.  

    (He) lives on the bank.  

    (He) lives on the bank of the lake.  

    (He) lives in (two) houses.  

    (He) lives on the moon.  

    (He) lives in the town of Charlottesville.
